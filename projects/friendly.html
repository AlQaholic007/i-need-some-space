Friend.ly

ML / Web

2019 - 2020
Technologies : NodeJS, Python, Tensorflow, Scikit-learn, Twitter OAuth2, Google OAuth2, Numpy, Pandas, IBM Watson
LINK

A modern friend recommender using text-mining and personality trait extraction.

The most integral part of any social networking platform is a Friend Recommendation System a.k.a FRS. Most of the current social networking platforms utilize a FRS based on homophily, meaning these systems will recommend people that you share common features or interests with.

While homophily based systems are accurate in circumstances when the common features include age, race, location, job, or lifestyle, it takes into little consideration the types of personalities.

Developed as our solution to IBM's online hackathon, Friend.ly is a social networking platform that uses a FRS based on the big-five personality traits model and hybrid filtering, in which we recommend friends based on the personality traits and harmony ratings given by the users.
